Daily Flow

The height of a single bar is the total number of people suffered from Coronavirus in Gibraltar and confirmed to be infected. It includes three parts: those who could or could not recover and those who are currently in the active phase of the disease.

New Confirmed Cases

This graph shows the number of new cases by day. The lightblue bars are the number of the new total confirmed cases appeared that day.

Daily Speed

This graph shows the speed of growth (in %) over time in Gibraltar. The only parameter here is the number of confirmed cases.

Note. When the speed is positive, the number of cases grows every day. The line going down means that the speed decreases, and while there may be more cases the next day, the disease spread is slowing down. If the speed goes below zero, that means that fewer cases registered today than yesterday.

Per capita values

Here, the number of confirmations and deaths per 1000 of population in Gibraltar is shown. These numbers is a better choice when comparing different countries than absolute numbers.
